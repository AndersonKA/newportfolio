Kaizening CTA Summary

What is this?

I did an internship for a wellness/lifestyle blog, and this was a redesign of the CTAs on their WordPress site.

Why did I make this?

The owner was using a default Mailchimp form for her email signup and contact form. She wanted to increase her mailing list's subscriber count.

What was my role?

I did the research and redesign myself, focusing on what competitors were doing, what the owner wanted to achieve, and the feedback I got from blog readers.

What was the process?

I talked with Lindsey about her goals and ideas for the form. She wanted something that fit better with the rest of her blog and that looked more appealing.

I did some competitive analysis of other lifestyle brand's forms to see what those sites were doing.

I created a few designs for the form and had the owner and three women who read lifestyle blogs look at them. One of the forms was the unanimous winner, so I used CSS to update the actual form in WordPress.

What was the result?

Within two months of the CTA redesign the email list had grown by 30%.

What did I learn?

Small changes can lead to big improvements.
