SpaceX Flight Booking Site Concept

In September of 2017 Elon Musk announced that SpaceX has plans for commercial flights using the BFR on Earth (you can watch their teaser video here) that will be able to make long distance flights much faster than they are now. When that happens they'll need a way for people to book flights, so this is a concept for their flight booking site.
