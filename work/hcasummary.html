HCA Healthcare Summary

What is this?

My current role is updating accessibility issues on HCA Healthcare's sites.

Why am I working on this?

Healthcare companies impact everyone's life, and they want to make sure their websites were available to everyone who needs to use them. I've been hired as a contractor for the remediation project.

What's my role?

I'm part of a team of 5 content specialists, 2 project managers, and a QA specialist who are all dedicated to this project.

On a regular basis I do things like:

  • Auditing images to make sure they have appropriate alt tags and making updates when they don't
  • Doing document checks on PDFs to make sure they have appropriate tags for screenreaders
  • Adding caption files to videos on YouTube
  • Training new members of the accessibility team
and I've also helped out with:
  • helpdesk tickets to make updates to the sites for marketing directors
  • a project called Find Care, which consolidates hundreds of pages on sites down to a smaller number to make it easier for users to find what they're looking for

What will the result be?

When the project ends we'll have updated hundreds of websites to make them more accessible.

What have I learned?

I know a lot more about digital accessibility (including the challenges and subjectivity that can go along with it), and I've become more sure that I love making the internet better for people.
