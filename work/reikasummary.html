Reika Summary

What is this?

Reika is an app that lets people organize their travel information easily. The focus was on creating a product that's simple to use and will make it less of a hassle to find the address of the hotel you're staying at when you're going through customs.

Why did I make this?

This is my capstone project for a UX bootcamp. I like traveling, and there's a lot of stuff to plan for international trips, so I wanted to make something that would help keep everything organized and in one place.

What was the result?

This is a class project, so it probably won't end up being a live app.

What was my role?

I did everything myself, from the early research to the designs. (But lots of nice people answered my questions and gave me great feedback.)

What did I learn?

I learned that keeping things simple is really helpful and that little things matter. I also found out that there's an app called TripIt that you can use for travel organization in real life.
