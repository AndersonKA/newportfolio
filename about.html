I've always been curious about people, and I'm an introvert, so observing comes naturally to me. I also love the internet (I went online for the first time in science class when I was 14 and fell in love right away!). When I was 15 my best friend and I made terrible *N Sync fan sites on Angelfire. I got a degree in anthropology because I love learning about people and cultures, and after years of working in unrelated jobs I decided to digitalize my skills, so in 2017 I went to a coding bootcamp for front end development.

Now I'm combining my curiousity about people with my love of the internet and becoming a UX designer. I'm a student at Springboard, an online UX design bootcamp where I'm gaining research and design skills.

I’ve been nerding out about space since I was 10. I’m a member of the Planetary Society, I get texts from NASA to let me know when I can see the ISS fly over Nashville, and I’ve met Buzz Aldrin!
