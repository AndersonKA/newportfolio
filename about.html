I've always been curious about people, and I'm an introvert, so observing comes naturally to me. I also love the internet (I went online for the first time in science class when I was 14 and was smitten right away!). After that my best friend and I made terrible *N Sync fan sites on Angelfire (complete with guestbooks and glittery banners).

I got a degree in anthropology because I love learning about people and cultures, and after years of working in unrelated jobs I decided to digitalize my skills, so in 2017 I went to a coding bootcamp for front end development.

Now I'm combining my curiosity about people with my love of the internet and becoming a UX designer. I'm a student at Springboard, an online UX design bootcamp where I'm gaining research and design skills.

I’m a Myers-Briggs INFP (I care about the work I do and love coming up with win-win ideas), an Enneagram 4w5 (I’m a creative problem solver who’s great at focusing on tasks), and a Hogwarts Ravenclaw (I like learning new things).
