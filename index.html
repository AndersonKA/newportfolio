How I Got Here

I've always been curious about people. I got a degree in anthropology because learning about humans comes naturally for me.

I had a job working on a helpdesk where I got calls and emails from people who weren't able to understand how to use a third-party database site. I spent hundreds of hours training them, and knew there had to be a way to make sites that were intuitive and easy to use. I left my job and went to a bootcamp for front-end development, but while I was there I realized it's not a developer's job to care about the user!

Cue my love for inclusive design! It takes my interest in people and mixes in technology and creativity - it's the perfect fit!

I've worked at a range of companies: one with two employees and one with over one hundred thousand, brand new startups and companies that have been around since before I was born, in fields from healthcare to publishing. I can get the work done in any kind of environment.
